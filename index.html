🏛️ About CLRLC

The Center for Low-Resource Languages and Cultures is dedicated to preserving and promoting linguistic heritage through technology. Mama Coco Bot is our flagship initiative — bringing ancestral knowledge to the digital age.

🌍 Why It Matters

Every two weeks, a language disappears from the earth. With Mama Coco Bot, we use AI not to replace tradition, but to carry it forward — ensuring that the stories our grandmothers told never fade into silence.
