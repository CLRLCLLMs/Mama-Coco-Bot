✦ About the Project ✦

What Is Mama Coco Bot?

Mama Coco Bot is an AI-powered cultural companion designed to preserve and share African oral traditions — folktales, proverbs, songs, idioms and ancestral wisdom — in their original languages.

Why It Matters

Every two weeks, a language disappears from the earth. AI has traditionally ignored low-resource languages. We use it instead to carry them forward — ensuring that the stories our grandmothers told never fade into silence, and that proverbs passed across generations remain alive.

Mama Coco Bot is Coming

The interactive AI companion is currently in development and will launch once our community archive reaches the depth needed to represent each language authentically. The more you contribute now, the sooner she arrives. Help us build her knowledge — submit a proverb, idiom or dataset today.

Why Your Contribution Matters

AI models are only as culturally aware as the data they are trained on. Most African languages are drastically underrepresented in existing AI datasets. Every proverb or idiom you submit directly shapes what Mama Coco Bot knows, how authentic her responses feel, and how many people can connect with their heritage through her.
